About the Writer
Vikram was born in Michigan, USA but moved to Bangalore, India for the majority of his childhood where he absorbed the culture of tech-entreprenurship. He lived a sheltered life and always had other people with more previldge to compare himself to but only later realized that he was among the most previleged people in India, making him extremely ambitious yet humble.

Strengths
Since I am personally on a financial literacy journey, all the content seems authentic. Since the purpose of the pieces are just to share information and increase financial literacy in the community without any alterior motive. Another strength is that the communication is confident which increases the credibility.

Improvement Opportunities
The production quality of my video can be improved by removing background sounds of the heating system running. I could also record a better final take that requires less cuts when I'm less nervous speaking to a camera. I would also consider changing the graphic design layout of design elements to make the information more digestable.
Lastly, it is unlikely for a viewer of the video to actually take action when watching the video, so my call to action could be stronger.
|
Lessons Learned
I often spent too much time perfecting the little things that eventually didn't even make it to the final essay, with improved time management skills I would be able to have better results to show for the time invested in these projects. I would spend more time learning from the mistakes of others by consuming more content from influencers about the mistakes they made on their journey.
Writing Process
I always start a piece from an outline of the topics I want to cover and then go into details about each topic to discuss. This works in my favor to make focused and clear written pieces. I did not follow this for my Youtube video as I was abstracting the content from the essay I had written which caused me to lose focus at times and cause me to ramble while recording the video, in order to change this the next time I would have clear points noted down before recording.
