Vikram Mishra is a dynamic entrepreneur and industrial designer who has seamlessly transitioned from student to AgTech innovator. As the CEO and co-founder of ZAPTrack, Vikram is leading the charge in revolutionizing organic farming through cutting-edge robotics and automation.

Background and Education

Vikram's journey in innovation began early, founding his first startup at the age of 13. He is currently completing his Bachelor of Science in Industrial Design at the University of Cincinnati, where he is also a Next Innovation Scholar.

Professional Experience

ZAPTrack Leadership

As the Chief Executive Officer of ZAPTrack since April 2024, Vikram is spearheading the development of AgTech robots designed to automate organic food production.

Previous Ventures

Prior to ZAPTrack, Vikram explored various facets of technology and design:

  • Weedless Robotics, experimenting with autonomous weeding robots and custom-built drones equipped with AI algorithms for field analysis.
  • Hardware Consultant for Founders, Inc., scaling prototype production for AI-embedded toys.
  • Internships at P&G and Sensio Enterprises, providing hands-on experience in industrial design and design for manufacturing.

Vision and Impact

Vikram's mission with ZAPTrack is to scale organic farming through robotics, addressing the challenges faced by mid-size organic farmers.
