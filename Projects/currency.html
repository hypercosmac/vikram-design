Currency Authenticator

Computer Vision

Watch a Live Demo

The machine is made for indivduals with low or no vision to aid with verification and identification of currency notes.

This prototype was made to work with Indian currency. It used two cameras along with 3 different light modes to accurately verify the currency notes inserted in the machine.

I chose to use a Raspberry Pi 4 for this prototype as it was a Proof of Concept - The performance could further be improved through adding a Neural Compute Stick to this set up.
